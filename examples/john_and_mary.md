# John and Mary

## Scene 1

John meets Mary and asks:
- What's your name?

Mary answers:
- My name is Mary.

Mary then asks:
- And what is your name?

John answers:
- My name is John.

Mary then says:
- Nice to meet you, John!

John answers: 
- Nice to meet you, Mary!
